Shelby Protest

Date & Time: 2025-02-23 10 AM - 1 PM

Location: Shelby, NC

Address: 108 East Warren Street, Shelby, NC

Meeting Location: LilyBean Coffee & Creamery

Description: This is not just a simple protest, it is a way to spread positivity, awareness and educating those who are unaware of the resources and opportunities available for them. It will begin at 1PM at LilyBean Coffee and Creamer. There will be table with information sheets, and a place to drop off donations for the community pantry and fridge for those less fortunate.
